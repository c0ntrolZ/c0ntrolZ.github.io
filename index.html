About

I am a second-year Master's student in Computer Science at EPFL (GPA 5.3/6), with a background spanning machine learning, cryptography, and distributed systems. My research interest lies in understanding and improving the reasoning capabilities of large language models — including evaluation methodology, alignment, and the design of systems that can generalize robustly.
